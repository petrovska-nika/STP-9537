Tips for Players

  1. Focus on clearing obstacles before creating combos.
  2. Try each decor option before confirming—some unlock special bonuses
  3. Log in daily for timed rewards and boosters
  4. Watch optional ads for extra coins or free lives
  5. Replay levels to collect missed stars and improve your design budget

Design success is about both vision and strategy—master both for the full experience.
