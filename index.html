About the Game

Match & Design is a game that blends match-3 puzzles with interactive interior design. As players solve vibrant, strategic block puzzles, they earn rewards to decorate, renovate, and personalize virtual spaces.
Each design decision—whether choosing a sleek modern sofa or painting the walls in soft pastels—unlocks new emotional rewards. The gameplay is balanced for both casual fun and thoughtful challenge, making it perfect for players who love to relax and create.

Match & Design follows a classic match-3 puzzle mechanic where players swap colored blocks to align three or more in a row. Each level has its own unique goal, such as reaching a score, clearing obstacles, or collecting specific pieces. As you progress, you’ll encounter new block types, obstacles like frozen tiles, and advanced combos. Completing puzzle levels earns stars, which you use to unlock new design options and tasks. Some levels also introduce special items and boosters to help you solve them faster. The puzzles are designed to be satisfying yet increasingly strategic as the game unfolds.

In Match & Design, you can decorate a variety of living spaces such as bedrooms, kitchens, patios, and entire apartments. Each space is broken down into multiple elements—walls, flooring, furniture, lighting, and decorative pieces. You choose from several style options for each item, giving you full control over the interior design. The result is a personalized and visually expressive space that reflects your taste. New design zones unlock as you progress through puzzle levels. Over time, you’ll have the chance to build an entire collection of custom-designed rooms.

Players earn a range of rewards including coins, boosters, stars, and sometimes decorative items for special events. Completing levels grants stars, which are used to unlock design tasks. Coins are used to purchase extra moves or power-ups during puzzles. Daily logins, streaks, and special challenges often provide bonus rewards that enhance your experience. Occasionally, limited-time events offer exclusive decor styles that you won’t find anywhere else. All rewards are designed to support both puzzle progress and your design creativity. You can always revisit earlier rooms to use new items you’ve unlocked.

Yes, absolutely. Match & Design allows you to revisit any previously decorated room and change your selections. If you want to try a different couch, wallpaper, or color scheme, simply tap on the element and reselect your option. There’s no penalty for changing your mind, and no extra cost for reselecting previously unlocked items. This flexibility encourages experimentation and creativity without stress. It also means you can return to earlier spaces with new ideas or decor options that may not have been available before. Your home is always evolving—just like your style.

If you lose a level, you’ll lose one life. Once your lives are depleted, you can wait for them to regenerate over time, which happens automatically. Alternatively, you can watch an optional ad to gain a free life or use in-game currency to refill your lives instantly. Some daily bonuses and events also reward lives or life refills. Managing your lives strategically can help you tackle harder levels more effectively. We aim to balance difficulty and accessibility, so players always have a way to continue without feeling blocked.
