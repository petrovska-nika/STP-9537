About the Game

Match & Design is a game that blends match-3 puzzles with interactive interior design. As players solve vibrant, strategic block puzzles, they earn rewards to decorate, renovate, and personalize virtual spaces.
Each design decision—whether choosing a sleek modern sofa or painting the walls in soft pastels—unlocks new emotional rewards. The gameplay is balanced for both casual fun and thoughtful challenge, making it perfect for players who love to relax and create.

 Key Features

  • Hundreds of Puzzle Levels

    Carefully crafted match-3 boards with increasing complexity.

  • Design-Driven Progression

    Each win earns you the chance to style a part of your home.

  • Custom Interiors

How the Game Works

  • 1

    Match Blocks

    Solve match-3 puzzles by aligning 3 or more of the same tiles.

  • 2

    Earn Stars

    Every completed puzzle gives you stars used to unlock design actions.

  • 3

    Redecorate Spaces

    Choose between three decor styles for each item—your pick shapes the final look.

  • 4

    Progress Through Rooms

    Unlock kitchens, living rooms, outdoor patios, and more.

  • 5

    Collect Bonuses

    Special puzzle challenges and design tasks yield coins, boosters, and gifts.

Design success is about both vision and strategy—master both for the full experience.

Tips for Players

  1. Focus on clearing obstacles before creating combos.
  2. Try each decor option before confirming—some unlock special bonuses
  3. Log in daily for timed rewards and boosters
  4. Watch optional ads for extra coins or free lives
  5. Replay levels to collect missed stars and improve your design budget
