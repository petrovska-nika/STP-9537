About the Game

Match & Design is a game that blends match-3 puzzles with interactive interior design. As players solve vibrant, strategic block puzzles, they earn rewards to decorate, renovate, and personalize virtual spaces.
Each design decision—whether choosing a sleek modern sofa or painting the walls in soft pastels—unlocks new emotional rewards. The gameplay is balanced for both casual fun and thoughtful challenge, making it perfect for players who love to relax and create.
